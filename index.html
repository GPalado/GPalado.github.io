About Me

Hi, I'm Gabrielle Palado. I am currently a second year Software Engineering student at the Victoria University of Wellington. I was first introduced to programming in my third year of high school when I chose Digital Technologies as one of my subjects. I'd always loved puzzles and riddles, and learning how to solve problems and create something out of nothing using code sparked my interest in pursuing a software engineering career.

Outside academics I play golf competitively, and have been representing my club as well as the Wellington district for the past few years. I am also an avid musician; I arrange music for my father's choir and, when sparked with inspiration, write my own music as well.
